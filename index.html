Want More Details?

The conference will be taking place at George Mason University with the participants staying in the dorms and everyone hacking together in communal areas. We'll be working on projects that help our communities. We are currently picking and voting on topics here.

Sign Up for Ruby for Good

We Meet Often. Come Join Us.

View our Meetups site for our next location and time. We meet every two weeks at GMU in Arlington, Virginia near the Clarendon Metro station. We are a great group that loves new members so stop by!

We Discuss Things Online. Join and Introduce Yourself.

Can't make it to a meetup? No problem. We collaborate on projects, ask for help, talk about interestin things, and post funny pictures in our IRC room #arlingtonruby on freenode.
