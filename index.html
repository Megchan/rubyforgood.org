Want More Details?

The conference will be taking place at George Mason University with the participants staying in the dorms and everyone hacking together in communal areas. We'll be working on projects that help our communities. We are currently picking and voting on topics here.

What to bring?

You just need to bring yourself, your laptop, and whatever you plan on wearing. You will get your own dorm room complete with linens, pillow, blanket and towels so you don't have to worry about hauling that stuff with you. We'll also be supplying the food, drinks, and fun atmosphere. If you have a particular board game or card game that you would like to bring please do so because we can only guarantee that we'll have the equipment needed to play Artemis.

Anything else?

Even though the conference runs from August 1st through August 3rd, attendees are welcome to arrive on July 31st and stay through August 4th if it helps with flights or if you just want more time in DC. There will be food available for those coming early and staying late, just provide a heads up so there is enough food available.

What does it cost?

Currently, tickets are going cost about $200 and that is all inclusive. With your ticket comes a room with a bed and bathroom (note: some rooms have shared bathrooms), linens for your bed and bathroom as well as food and a hackfest t-shirt and stickers. Preparing food is considered a team building exercise so all meals as well as the clean up after the meals will be done by attendees. We are currently in discussion with some sponsors and hope to get the ticket price down. Hopefully we will have tickets up for sale by the end of May.
